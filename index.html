Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are used in your jewellery?⌄

High-grade brass and alloy bases with premium anti-tarnish gold and rhodium plating, set with hand-picked cubic zirconia and imported crystal stones. Every finish is nickel-free and skin-safe.

Do you offer custom jewellery design?⌄

Yes. Our design studio takes limited custom commissions for bridal sets and occasion wear. Book a private consultation at the showroom and we will bring your piece to life.

How do I determine my ring size?⌄

Use our printable ring sizer, or measure a ring you already own against our size chart. In doubt between two sizes? Choose the larger, resizing within 15 days is complimentary.

Do your pieces come with a certificate of authenticity?⌄

Every piece ships with a Velmont authenticity card, a care guide, and our signature packaging. Registered pieces are also covered by lifetime shine care.

What are the shipping and return options?⌄

Free insured shipping across India, with cash on delivery available. Returns are free for 15 days with doorstep pick-up, and exchanges are processed within 48 hours.
